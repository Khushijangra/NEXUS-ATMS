# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Give and accept feedback professionally.
- Focus discussions on technical issues and solutions.
- Assume good intent.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or intimidation.
- Deliberate disruption of project collaboration.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards.

## Reporting conduct issues

Report incidents privately to the maintainer. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled confidentially to the extent possible.

## Consequences

Maintainers may warn, temporarily restrict, or permanently remove participants who violate this code.
